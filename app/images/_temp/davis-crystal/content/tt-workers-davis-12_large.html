TYLER, TX - MAY 7: Wayne Davis' gravesite at the Bascom Cemetery in Tyler, TX, on Wednesday, May 7, 2014. Wayne Davis was killed in an auto collision in 2012. After winning court battles against an insurance company to pay the death benefits the law said she was owed, Wayne's wife, Crystal Davis and their two children are now in turn being sued by the insurance company to cease payments and return the money.
